Koenigsegg Agera RS

The Hypercar King

Overview

The Koenigsegg Agera RS is one of the fastest and most powerful hypercars ever built. It combines lightweight carbon fiber design with a twin‑turbo V8 engine to deliver unmatched performance.

Key Specifications

  • Engine: 5.0L Twin‑Turbo V8
  • Power: 1176 hp
  • 0–100 km/h: ~2.8s
  • Top Speed: 447 km/h (recorded)
